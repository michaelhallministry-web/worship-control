Built for Worship Teams

Everything you need to lead the presentation without living in the booth.

Worship Control brings the most important ProPresenter controls into one focused, easy-to-use desktop experience.

Fast Setup

Connect in seconds.

Enter the ProPresenter host IP address and API port, save the connection, and start controlling your presentation right away.

✓Uses ProPresenter's documented HTTP API
✓Local network connection with no cloud dependency
✓Designed for fast reconnects and dependable use

Playlist Navigation

Your entire service at a glance.

See every presentation, song, message, video, and live action in the active playlist. Jump where you need to go without breaking the flow of worship.

✓Full playlist visibility
✓Visual thumbnails and item durations
✓Fast access to the next presentation item

Slide Ruler

See every slide before you advance.

The visual slide ruler gives you thumbnail access to the active presentation, helping you move quickly and confidently through songs, sermons, and media.

✓Visual slide thumbnails
✓Jump directly to any slide
✓Keep the active slide clearly identified

Current and Next

Always know what is live—and what is next.

See the current audience slide and the upcoming slide together, so every transition feels prepared instead of rushed.

✓Current slide preview
✓Next slide preview
✓Clear screen and resolution information

Live Stream Control

Start your stream. Stay focused.

Load ProPresenter stream or recording profiles, monitor live status, and start or stop the selected profile from Worship Control.

✓Stream and recording profile selection
✓Live online or offline status
✓Real-time capture duration

Stage Communication

Communicate without interrupting the service.

Send messages directly to the stage display for worship teams, speakers, and volunteers—then clear them with one click.

✓Remote stage messaging
✓Large message editing area
✓Fast send and clear controls

Transport and Timeline

Navigate with speed and confidence.

Move forward or backward through slides and control presentation timelines without hunting through multiple windows.

✓Previous and next slide controls
✓Play, pause, and rewind attached tracks
✓Everything needed to keep the service moving
Current network recommendation: Early testing has shown the strongest connection when the ProPresenter host computer is connected by Ethernet. The Worship Control computer may connect by Ethernet or Wi-Fi on the same local network. Additional network configurations remain under active testing.
